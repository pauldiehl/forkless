# Man vs Health — Capacities

## What We Can Do Right Now (Phase 1)

### Conversations
- Health intake conversations (discover what the user needs)
- Nutrition guidance and meal planning concepts
- Allergy education and immunotherapy awareness
- Sleep optimization recommendations
- General wellness Q&A grounded in our knowledge base

### Artifacts We Can Generate
- Personalized health plans (HTML rendered in Transaction Layer)
- Nutrition guides and meal framework suggestions
- Symptom tracking templates
- Doctor visit preparation guides (questions to ask)
- Progress summaries

### What We Can't Do Yet
- Schedule appointments
- Connect with real providers
- Process payments
- Access external health data (lab results, wearables)
- Video consultations

## Limitations to Be Honest About

- We're an education and guidance platform, not a medical practice
- Our knowledge is broad but not infinitely deep — we'll say when something is outside our expertise
- Personalization improves over time as the profig grows — early conversations will be more general
- We can't verify the accuracy of what users tell us about their health
