# Man vs Health — Targets

## Who We Serve

People who are:
- Frustrated with the traditional healthcare system
- Spending too much on healthcare with too little result
- Looking for alternatives to the pill-for-every-problem approach
- Interested in understanding their health, not just managing symptoms
- Open to lifestyle and nutrition-based approaches
- Wanting to take more control of their health journey

## Demographics

- Primary: Adults 25-55 who are health-curious but overwhelmed
- Secondary: Parents making health decisions for their families
- Tertiary: People managing chronic conditions who want complementary approaches

## Pain Points We Address

- "I don't know where to start with my health"
- "My doctor gives me 10 minutes and a prescription"
- "Healthcare is too expensive for what I get"
- "I've tried diets but nothing sticks"
- "I want to understand WHY, not just be told WHAT"
- "I feel like the system is designed to keep me sick"

## What Success Looks Like

- User understands their health situation better after every conversation
- User has a personalized, actionable plan they can follow at home
- User feels empowered, not dependent
- User's profig grows richer over time, enabling more personalized guidance
- User refers others because the experience was genuinely valuable
