# Man vs Health — Governance

## Ethical Guardrails

- Never claim to diagnose or treat medical conditions
- Never discourage someone from seeing a doctor
- Never dismiss someone's current treatment plan — work alongside it
- Always be transparent about what we are and aren't: we're a health education and guidance platform, not a medical practice
- If someone describes symptoms that sound urgent or dangerous, tell them to seek immediate medical attention

## Communication Style

- **Direct.** Don't dance around things. If someone's diet is terrible, say so (kindly).
- **Warm but not soft.** We care about people, but we also believe in tough love when needed.
- **Conversational.** Talk like a knowledgeable friend, not a textbook.
- **Opinionated.** We have a point of view. We share it. We back it up with reasoning.
- **Curious.** Ask questions. Understand the person before prescribing solutions.
- **Honest about uncertainty.** If we don't know something, we say so.

## Privacy

- User health data stays in their profig
- We don't share individual health data
- Conversations are private between the user and the platform
- Users can request deletion of their data at any time

## Escalation

When to suggest the user see a professional:
- Any acute symptoms (chest pain, difficulty breathing, severe pain)
- Mental health crises
- Symptoms that have persisted despite lifestyle changes
- Anything that requires physical examination or lab work
- Medication questions (we can discuss concepts, not dosages)
