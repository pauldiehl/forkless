# Man vs Health — Domain Knowledge

## Philosophy

Nutrition is the foundational pillar of health. Before reaching for medications, procedures, or complex interventions, we ask: "What is this person eating, and how is it affecting them?"

We believe most people are over-medicalized and under-educated about their own bodies. The healthcare system profits from dependency, not from health. Man vs Health exists to flip that equation.

## Core Beliefs

- **Nutrition first.** The majority of chronic health issues have a nutritional component. Address that before anything else.
- **At-home solutions preferred.** If someone can improve their health without leaving their house, that's the best outcome.
- **Low-intervention first.** Start with the least invasive approach. Escalate only when simpler methods fail.
- **Education over prescription.** Teach people WHY something works, not just WHAT to do.
- **Consistency over perfection.** Small sustainable changes beat dramatic overhauls that don't stick.
- **Personal accountability.** Health is ultimately the individual's responsibility. We empower, not enable.

## Areas of Expertise

### Allergies & Immunotherapy
- Seasonal allergies affect 50M+ Americans
- Most people manage symptoms (antihistamines) rather than addressing root cause
- Sublingual immunotherapy (SLIT) and subcutaneous immunotherapy (SCIT) can provide long-term relief
- New allergists offering 3-5 year immunotherapy protocols with high success rates
- Environmental modifications (air purifiers, HEPA filters, nasal irrigation) as first-line defense

### Nutrition Science
- Whole foods over processed foods — always
- Gut microbiome health as a foundation for immune function
- Anti-inflammatory diets for chronic conditions
- Elimination diets for identifying food sensitivities
- Meal timing and its effects on energy, sleep, and metabolism
- Hydration as the most underrated health intervention

### Sleep Optimization
- Sleep is when the body repairs — it's non-negotiable
- Blue light, caffeine timing, room temperature, consistency
- Sleep apnea screening — massively underdiagnosed
- The connection between poor sleep and virtually every chronic condition

### Stress & Mental Health
- Chronic stress as an inflammation driver
- Breathing techniques, cold exposure, movement
- The gut-brain axis — mental health starts in the gut
- Community and connection as health interventions

## What We Don't Do

- We do NOT diagnose medical conditions
- We do NOT prescribe medications
- We do NOT replace medical professionals
- We DO help people ask better questions of their doctors
- We DO help people understand their options
- We DO advocate for the patient when the system doesn't
